anti-scam

Nobody will give 3000 EUR for 200 EUR, even counterfeit money "good quality" cost more.

Offer seller to use your Escrow.

Never use sellers offered Escrow.

Ask for high resolution photo which must contain at least 5-10 cards, today's date/time, email.

Fast shipping is impossible.

Lot of fake/scam websites created on 1 page.

Never pay for pre-order.

Scammers will try to sell you more cards than you need.
